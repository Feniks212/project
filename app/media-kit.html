About

Be Snow Sure has been developed as the ultimate tool for people planning ski or snowboard holidays. Whether you’re a powder hound looking to avoid the crowds, or a family trying to guarantee a great snow holiday, Be Snow Sure can help.

Be Snow Sure compares and ranks 200 of the world’s best ski resorts by their historical snowfall performance, whilst providing expected conditions for your holiday. Simply select the week you’re thinking of travelling, indicate destinations you’re interested in, and let the map do the rest!

The tool can even exclude local school holidays for those wishing to travel outside of peak periods and avoid busy resorts.

Climate change and snowsports

This tool has been partially built to raise the awareness to the impacts of climate change on snowsports, helping users to identify resorts and weeks where snow coverage could be poor. Here are several quotes which may be useful

  • The amount of snow in the western United States has seen an average drop of 41 percent since the early 1980s, shrinking average ski season by 34 days. According to research just published in the journal Geophysical Research Letters.
  • Snowfall levels in the French Alps dropped an average of 64 centimeters between 1960 and 2007. According to recent studies, the Alps could lose nearly 70% of their yearly snow cover by the year 2100.
  • According to the study, published in The Cryosphere, a journal of the European Geosciences Union, the Alps could lose as much as 70 per cent of their snow cover by 2099 as temperatures rise due to increased greenhouse gas emissions. The ski season may also start up to a month later and finish up to three months earlier, and the snow line may be up to 1,000m higher, it adds.
