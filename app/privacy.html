Privacy and Data

Privacy & Cookies

The website may use tracking technologies such as cookies to understand usage and performance. Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to a computer’s hard disk for record keeping purposes. Most web browsers are set to accept tracking technologies such as cookies or web beacons. These tracking technologies do not themselves personally identify users, although they do identify a user’s browser.

Snowfall Data Used

The Be Snow Sure application uses historical snowfall data taken from Snow Forecast. Snow Forecast has provided the average weekly snowfall against months of the year for the last 11 years, and this has been aligned with Saturday - Friday weeks for the 2019/20 season.

  • Data used is based on mid-mountain snowfall statistics
  • Cumulative snowfall is total snowfall since start of November
  • Resorts are ranked based on total weekly snowfall in the chosen week

School Holiday Data

School and public holiday data has been taken from multiple online sources and resources. Open and closing date data has also been used to help users avoid choosing closed resorts during the week of their travel.
